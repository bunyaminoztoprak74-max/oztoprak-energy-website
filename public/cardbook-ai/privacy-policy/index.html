01

Overview

CardBook AI is developed and operated by Bünyamin Öztoprak, Türkiye. This Privacy Policy explains how CardBook AI collects, uses, stores and protects information when you use the application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The vast majority of your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Short version: Your business card data and contacts stay on your device. We use Google ML Kit for on-device OCR. We use RevenueCat only to manage your subscription status. We never sell your data.

02

Device Permissions We Request

CardBook AI requests the following device permissions. Each permission is used only for the stated purpose and is never used to collect data beyond what is described here.

Camera

Used to capture photos of business cards directly within the app. Images are processed on-device by Google ML Kit and are never uploaded to external servers.

Photo Library Access

Used to import existing photos of business cards from your device gallery. Access is read-only. Selected images are processed locally and not stored on any server.

Storage (Android)

Required on older Android versions to write exported CSV, Excel, and vCard files to your device's Downloads folder. We do not read any files outside the app's own directory.

Permission principle: We request permissions at the time you first use a feature that requires it, not at app launch. You can revoke any permission at any time through your device Settings → Apps → CardBook AI → Permissions.

03

OCR Processing — Google ML Kit

CardBook AI uses Google ML Kit Text Recognition to extract text from business card images. This processing is performed entirely on your device (on-device inference). The business card image and the extracted text are not transmitted to Google's servers or to our servers during OCR processing.

  • ML Kit's on-device model runs locally — no internet connection is required for OCR
  • Business card images used for OCR are not retained after text extraction unless you explicitly save the card
  • Google ML Kit may collect anonymous usage statistics about API usage. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details: policies.google.com/privacy
04

Local Database Storage

All contact cards you save in CardBook AI are stored in a local database on your device. We use a secure local database (SQLite / Room) to store:

  • Business card contact details (name, company, phone, email, address, website, notes)
  • Card images you choose to save
  • Tags, folders, and organizational metadata you create
  • Scan history and import records

This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly use an export feature (see Section 5) or synchronization features (if enabled). We do not have access to your locally stored contacts. All locally stored data is protected by Android's built-in application sandbox and encrypted transport is used whenever data is transmitted.

Backup: If you have Android Auto Backup enabled in your device settings, your app data may be backed up to your Google Drive under your Google account. This is controlled by you and Google, not by CardBook AI. You can disable this in Android settings.

05

Data Export — CSV, Excel & vCard

CardBook AI allows you to export your contact data in multiple formats for portability and backup. All exports are generated locally on your device and saved to your chosen storage location.

CSV Export

Exports all contact fields to a comma-separated values file. Saved to your device's Downloads folder or a location you choose. Not transmitted anywhere.

Excel Export (.xlsx)

Exports your contacts to Microsoft Excel format with formatted columns. Generated locally on your device. Not uploaded to any server.

vCard Import / Export

Import .vcf contact files from your device or export individual cards as .vcf files to share or back up contacts. All processing is local. Import reads only the file you select.

You are solely responsible for the exported files. Once exported, the files are under your control and subject to the privacy settings of the platform or app you use to share them.

06

QR Code Sharing

CardBook AI can generate a QR code containing your contact information (vCard data) for sharing with others. The QR code is generated locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers to generate the QR code.

  • QR codes encode contact data in standard vCard format
  • The person scanning your QR code will receive the contact fields you chose to include
  • You control which fields are included in each QR code before sharing
  • QR codes can be saved as images or shared directly via your device's share sheet
07

Subscriptions & Billing

CardBook AI offers optional premium subscriptions processed through Google Play Billing and managed via RevenueCat.

Google Play Billing

All payments are processed by Google Play. CardBook AI never sees or stores your payment card details. Billing is handled entirely by Google. Google's Privacy Policy governs payment data.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscription status across devices. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous user ID and subscription status — not your name, email, or payment details. See: revenuecat.com/privacy

Subscription data shared with RevenueCat is limited to: a randomly generated user identifier, the app bundle ID, subscription product ID, and purchase validation token. No payment card data or personal identity information is shared with RevenueCat.

To cancel a subscription, go to Google Play → Subscriptions → CardBook AI → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

08

Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are integrated into CardBook AI. Each processes only the minimum data necessary for its function:

  • Google ML Kit (Text Recognition) — on-device OCR; no data transmitted for inference. Subject to Google ML Kit Terms
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing. Subject to Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Receives pseudonymous ID and subscription status only. Subject to RevenueCat Privacy Policy

We do not use advertising SDKs, social media SDKs, or behavioral analytics tools within CardBook AI.

09

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data or contact information to third parties for commercial or advertising purposes — ever. This applies to data you scan, import, manually enter, or export using CardBook AI.

We have no advertising business model. Our revenue comes solely from optional premium subscriptions processed through Google Play. Your contacts and scanned card data have no commercial value to us beyond powering the features you use.

10

Your Rights (GDPR & Data Subject Rights)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — You can view all data CardBook AI holds about you directly within the app at any time.
  • Right to Rectification — You can edit any contact card or personal data stored in the app.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten) — You can delete individual cards, groups, or all data from within the app. You can also uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.
  • Right to Data Portability — You can export all your data at any time via CSV, Excel, or vCard export.
  • Right to Object / Withdraw Consent — You can revoke device permissions (camera, photo library) at any time through your device settings.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint — You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Account and Data Deletion

To delete all your data from CardBook AI:

  • In-app deletion: Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete All Data. This permanently removes all cards, images, and settings from your device.
  • Email request: Contact us at info@oztoprakenerji.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will confirm deletion within 30 days.
  • Subscription data: RevenueCat anonymized records are deleted upon request to RevenueCat or automatically after the applicable retention period per their policy.

If you believe a child has used the app and provided personal data, please contact us at info@oztoprakenerji.com and we will take appropriate steps to delete the data.

Data Retention

Because most data is stored locally on your device, you control data retention entirely. Data is kept until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app.

  • Local card data — retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • RevenueCat subscription records — retained for the period required by their policy, typically up to 3 years for tax and legal compliance, then deleted
  • Google Play purchase tokens — retained by Google per their standard retention policy
