## Thin Content Policy

- Blog posts below 500 words should be expanded or merged.
- Similar programmatic pages should remain `noindex, follow` until manually edited.
- Category pages should stay indexable only if they list useful posts and have unique metadata.

## Ranking Strategy

Focus first on fewer, stronger URLs instead of hundreds of generated pages. For this domain stage, Google is more likely to index and rank high-trust service pages, detailed blog articles and real project/case-study content than broad programmatic combinations.
