# SEO Growth Sprint 2 - Oztoprak Energy

Date: 2026-06-17  
Primary domain: https://www.oztoprakenerji.com  
Goal: increase qualified organic traffic and generate consulting leads within 90 days.

## Executive Summary

Oztoprak Energy already has a strong bilingual Next.js foundation: localized routes, service pages, blog content, related-content blocks, sitemap routes, footer service links, and contact CTAs. Sprint 2 should not expand the sitemap with more weak programmatic URLs. The next SEO gain should come from strengthening a smaller group of commercial-intent pages and making them easier for Google and buyers to understand.

The highest-value opportunity is to build five lead-focused service assets around:

- Owner's Engineering / Isveren Muhendisligi
- Technical Due Diligence / Teknik Durum Tespiti
- HES Performance Analysis / HES Performans Analizi
- Industrial Energy Cost Optimization / Endustriyel Enerji Maliyet Optimizasyonu
- Electricity Bill Analysis / Elektrik Faturasi Analizi

Important duplicate-risk note: the site already has Owner's Engineering, technical audit, energy audit, hydropower, solar, EPC advisory and investment advisory pages. New Sprint 2 pages should either expand existing canonical service pages or be created as clearly differentiated sub-services with unique search intent. Do not create two pages targeting the same exact keyword family.
